Certification and Accreditation


9001:2015 - Quality Management System Certification

ISO 9001:2015 is defined as the international standard that specifies requirements for a quality management system (QMS) in an organization; and is the world’s most popular quality improvement standard. It helps organizations organize processes, improve the efficiency of processes, and enhance customer satisfaction.

ISO 27001:2013 - Information Security Management System Certification

ISO/IEC 27001:2013 is the leading international standard for information security management (ISMS). It specifies the requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining and continually improving an information security management system within the context of the organization. It also includes requirements for the assessment and treatment of information security risks tailored to the needs of the organization.

ISO 45001:2018 - Occupational Health and Safety Management System Certification

ISO 45001:2018 specifies requirements for an occupational health and safety (OH&S) management system. It is designed to enable organisations to control risks and improve performance in the area of OH&S. It places a proactive and preventative emphasis on risk-control factors by identifying and assessing the likelihood and severity of hazards in the workplace.

ISO 14001:2015 - Environmental Management System

ISO 14001:2015 specifies the requirements for an environmental management system that an organization can use to enhance its environmental performance. ISO 14001:2015 is intended for use by an organization seeking to manage its environmental responsibilities in a systematic manner that contributes to the environmental pillar of sustainability.

How We Do What We Do

Below are some of the Tender related activities which we can help you with.

Tender Analysis

We assist with the review of tender submissions against weighted average evaluation criteria to ensure you are ‘comparing apples with apples’ and incorporating all elements of tender responses.

Bid Preparation

We can help in preparing the most accurate and competitive tenders; from reviewing the Contract and client amendments, to ensuring that the best commercially viable bid is submitted in line with you fully understanding your contractual obligations

Pricing Strategy

Getting the Bid Price Right is first step in winning the project, we help you in selecting and bidding using the right pricing strategy which is neither simple nor easy as there is a need to remain competitive while making sure margins are also maintained.

Bid Submission

A good bid management and Submission process should be viewed as conducting the orchestra, ensuring that all other tendering activities such as planning and estimating are performing effectively and in conjunction with each other.
